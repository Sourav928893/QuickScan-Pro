# QuickScan Pro – QR & Barcode Scanner

<img src="qrpro.png" alt="App Logo" width="150"/>

**QuickScan Pro** is a professional Android app designed to **scan and generate QR codes and barcodes**. It is fast, intuitive, and fully offline, making it ideal for personal and business use.

---

## **Features**

### QR / Barcode Scanner
- Real-time camera scanning
- Supports URLs, text, phone numbers, emails, and product codes
- Flashlight toggle for low-light conditions
- Copy, open, or share scanned results instantly

### QR Generator
- Generate QR codes for text, URLs, phone, and email
- Save QR codes locally on your device
- Share QR codes via WhatsApp, Email, or other social apps

### Scan History
- Stores the last 30 scans locally using SQLite
- Easy access to previous scans
- Tap to copy, share, or re-scan codes

### User Interface
- Modern Material Design UI
- Light and Dark mode support
- Smooth animations for all interactions
- Responsive layout for all device sizes

### Privacy & Security
- All scan data is stored locally on your device
- No collection of personal identifiers
- Permissions handled responsibly (camera, storage)
- Full [Privacy Policy](Privacy%20Policy%20for%20QuickScan%20Pro.docx)
